🧀 Paneer Curry

← Back to Dinner

📝 Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp cream or yogurt
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt and fresh coriander to garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Stir in all spices and mix well.
  5. Add paneer cubes, cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add cream/yogurt, simmer for another 2–3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve with roti or rice.

⏱️ Details

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 2
