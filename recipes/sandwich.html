🥪 Grilled Sandwich

← Back to Lunch

📝 Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 onion, sliced
  • 1/4 tomato, sliced
  • 1/4 cucumber, sliced
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, chaat masala to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Spread butter on both bread slices.
  2. Layer veggies and cheese between slices.
  3. Sprinkle spices on top before closing the sandwich.
  4. Grill on a sandwich press or in a pan until golden and crispy.
  5. Slice diagonally and serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

⏱️ Details

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 1–2
