🍚 Veg Fried Rice

← Back to Dinner

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: spring onion for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok or pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add all chopped vegetables and stir-fry for 3–4 minutes.
  3. Add cooked rice and toss well with the veggies.
  4. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry everything for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

⏱️ Details

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Servings: 2
