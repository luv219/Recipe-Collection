🍌 Banana Smoothie

← Back to Breakfast

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt or milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 3–4 ice cubes
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Peel the banana and cut it into chunks.
  2. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  3. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass, sprinkle cinnamon if desired, and serve chilled.

⏱️ Details

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: –
  • Servings: 1
