🍝 Veggie Pasta

← Back to Lunch

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 cup penne or fusilli pasta
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, zucchini)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree or sauce
  • Salt, pepper, and chili flakes to taste
  • Fresh basil or oregano (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic for 30 seconds.
  3. Add chopped veggies and stir-fry until just tender.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, spices, and herbs.
  5. Add pasta and toss well until coated and heated through.
  6. Serve warm with grated cheese or fresh basil.

⏱️ Details

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 2
