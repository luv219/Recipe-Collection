🌿 Mixed Dal Tadka

← Back to Dinner

📝 Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1/4 cup moong dal (yellow split lentils)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds, 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp ghee or oil
  • Salt and coriander leaves for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Wash and pressure-cook dals with turmeric and 2 cups water for 3–4 whistles.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan, add cumin, then garlic. Sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Add onion and sauté until golden. Then add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add chili powder and cooked dal. Mix well and simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander. Serve hot with rice or roti.

⏱️ Details

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 2–3
