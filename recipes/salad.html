Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Dressing of choice

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Mix in a large bowl.
  3. Add dressing just before serving and toss well.
