🧀 Cheese Omelette

← Back to Breakfast

📝 Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp butter or oil

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and cook undisturbed for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over half the omelette and fold the other half over it.
  5. Cook for another 1–2 minutes until cheese is melted. Serve hot.

⏱️ Details

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 1
